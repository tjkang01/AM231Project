Professors Park and Ragab are wonderfulboth are extremely knowledgeable and engaging lecturers. Any chance to take a course with Kary Park is well worth it! In particular, it is a great course to take for a pre-1800s requirement. The course challenges dominant narratives of the history of "Western science" and provides valuable opportunities to examine assumptions of continuities and discontinuities in traditional historiography.
An invigorating account of history, will likely change your view on things you haven't given much thought.
