Prof. Zeghal is fantastic! She generates enthusiasm for the subject and material and her seminars are always exciting.
This course is an excellent overview of Arabic intellectual history in the context of reform and revival movements throughout the Arab world. The professor comments extensively on the contents of the primary texts and provides useful historical background to their authors and social milieus. The class conversation is very stimulating and all the students have a keen interest in learning this nuances of the material.
