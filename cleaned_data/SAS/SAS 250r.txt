This class seems useful primarily for those who will go on to do more Sanskrit philosophy. Alternatively, this gives an interesting additional perspective on "Buddhism", namely, that one cannot expect questions of practical religion to always predominate in works written by "Buddhists".
Professor Patil is a fabulous teacher and conveys the integrity and depth of Indian philosophical traditions in a way that cuts through many misunderstandings and compels one to learn much more about these intellectual cultures!
