This course is an excellent opportunity to pursue your own research project with the support of an outstanding instructor and in the collegial company of other like-minded and motivated students.
Come with a research question, and you will gain much more from this course.
Definitely take 203a before taking this course.
You should have a paper in mind or in progress when you start this course. The assignments will be easier if you have some experience with the data you want to use and are taking this course to build your skills with a particular project in mind.
This class is a good amount of work, but well worth it!
