Students should be aware that they and their fellow students will be leading classes for two-thirds of the semester. The required readings will give good background for each week's topic, but the presentation in class is led by students and the material covered depends, to some extent, on the students' preparation and interests.
This class is very helpful if you are interested in writing a paper on the topic of family.
Soc 217 will give you a good overview of research that has been conducted on the sociology of the family worldwide (with slight extra focus on the USA).
Marty is a very effective teacher and knows a lot.
