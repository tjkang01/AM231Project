If you were ever interested in the diversity of language and content within a corpus generally seen as one, take this course and be amazed.
It provides a solid grounding in the various genres and "stop-and-go" nature of texts from throughout the Middle English period.
If you are interested in Middle English literature or in getting a broader picture of post-Conquest England for other reasons (Celtic, Anglo-Norman), I strongly recommend this course.
