Here's a great way to get an overview of what's going on in astronomy today.
Punchy packets of astrophysical topics and/or techniques whose introductory yet fundamental principles are each taught in 4 lectures. The beauty of this course lies in its diversity and flexibility -- choice of 6/12 modules for credit, wide-ranging topics of interest, exposure to different lecturers and styles, and no stringent assumption of specialized background knowledge -- aiding digestibility.
