This is a great course if you plan to conduct your own quantitative research in international relations.  It provides a good understanding of the potential pitfalls and options for solving them.
A tour through a variety of methodological techniques in modern international relations.  Allows for deep critiques and investigation of a limited number of papers.  While the individual methods themselves are not engaged in depth, it does demonstrate how to effectively critique methods papers.
