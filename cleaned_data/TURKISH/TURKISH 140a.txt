This class is excellent but quite a stretch, especially for those who are not doing their dissertation specifically on Ottoman history/literature.  But it's a very rich subject area and the enthusiasm of Professor Taskomur and other students helps.
You should know Arabic alphabet by heart and feel comfortable with the most of the variations of printed Arabic and Persian texts. Develop the cognitive flexibility when need arises to reconstruct missing dots in a text. Do not be mislead by your previous knowledge of Modern Turkish, Modern Persian and Modern Arabic. Be prepared to encounter numerous rhetoric devices!
