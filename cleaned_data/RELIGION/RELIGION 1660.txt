This is not a course about advancing academic prowess, but instead gets at the heart of what the scholastic life should be about: questioning your deepest assumptions, delving deeply into very different understandings about the self and the world, engaging directly with texts that contain profound insight.
If you have any interest at all in Hindu theology or even in understanding Hindu culture in general, this class is an absolute MUST!  It establishes ancient thought within the tradition and forms the foundation on which modern Hinduism is established.
