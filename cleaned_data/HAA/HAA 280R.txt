It is a workshop that you can explore freely and deeply into fascinating issues of Buddhist art. You will definitely be surprised and amazed by the imagination, sophistication, and beauty of the 5th century Chinese Buddhist art.
You will learn more than just the Buddhist art, but also the Buddhist mind encoded in the cave.
It's useful if you have very specific interests in the materials and if you would like to think conceptually about the relation of Buddhist thought to visual art.
