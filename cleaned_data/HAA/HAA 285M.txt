If you are at all interested in Indian culture then this course is a must.  From world-famous gems such as Ajanta and Ellora cave temples to the less well-know Virupaksha Temple at Pattadakal, this class will give you some terrific insights not just into the architecture and history behind them, but also the narratives contained within them.
This is an exciting journey through Indian's religious architecture.You would be fascinated by the incredible beauty and sophistication of this unique architecture system.
