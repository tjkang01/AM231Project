This class will give you an opportunity to explore time periods and areas about which (at times) little is known; it will also give you the chance to think about the ways we have of knowing about the past.
A really fun and stimulating course that allows you to learn in a dynamic, collaborative intellectual environment and experiment with new and innovative technologies that will change the way you approach the study of early African history.
Professor Blier is especially understanding as an interdisciplinary scholar and researcher, and perhaps working out the specificities of class requirements early in the semester would prove helpful. Negotiating various elements of the class is central, ALL OF WHICH are of value, only because it may otherwise not be possible to accomplish all in depth.
