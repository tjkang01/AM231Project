Helpful to have research projects and ideas already in progress on Pindar or Greek literature which one can bring to the class as a kind of workshop and improve upon.
This course boasts a very broad approach, and the professor is very willing to engage with a wide range of cultural products and scholarly methodologies.
Gregory Nagy is one of the most impressive professors you'll ever meet!
The discussion can spin off in all kinds of directions, so be ready for anything.  You may not agree with everything (or most things), but some of it will be food for thought.
The class is a must if you have interests in Pindar or Greek lyric poetry generally, and gives great opportunity to deepen familiarity with the text(s) and performances and to explore research interests.
